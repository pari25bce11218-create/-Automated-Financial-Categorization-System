PROJECT: Automated Financial Categorization System

This project is about managing your money. It uses Python and some other tools like Pandas and NumPy to take your bank statement and turn it into a report that's easy to understand. The main idea is to stop entering data by hand by using keywords to categorize what you spend your money on and highlight the things.
An Automated Approach to Understanding Where the Money Goes

I think we have all been in this situation: looking at our bank statement at the end of the month and wondering where our money went. Most banking apps just give us an idea like "Shopping" or "Other" which does not really help. The Project Report was started because I wanted to have control over my finances. I made a Python program that helps me understand my money better. This way I do not have to rely on banking apps that do not give me the information I need.
